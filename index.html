Privacy Policy

1. Who We Are

La Jolla Holistic Center, Inc. ("LJHC," "we," "our") is a California corporation located at 888 Prospect St, Ste 200, La Jolla, CA 92037. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information submitted through this website (lajollaholisticcenter.com).

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily submit through our contact form, which may include:

  • Name

  • Message content

3. How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through our contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry and, where applicable, to initiate a professional engagement. We do not use your information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or as necessary to deliver services you have requested (e.g., email service providers used to receive contact form submissions).

Inquiry data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was submitted, or as required by applicable law.

6. Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies or independent tracking technologies. We do not operate analytics or advertising tracking on this site.

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@lajollaholisticcenter.com.

8. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.
